Amenities

Akane Beach

Step outside onto Akane Beach. Our resort is only steps away for you and your family to have some fun on the best beach in Magnolia.

Swimming Pools

Take a swim at our large outdoor swimming pool on the south side or quietly relax at our comfort zone pool area at the west side.

Spa

Nothing beats a trip to the spa located on the east side. We provide services such as relaxing massages, saunas, more.

Restaurants

Visit our Beachside Restaurant serving delicious breakfast, lunch, and dinner or visit the Palm Tree Steakhouse for dinner.

The Beach Bar

Get your favorite cold beer or other alcoholic beverages at our outdoor bar. We are located on the south side of the resort.

Bisca's Cafe

Need a cup of coffee? Come on in to get freshly made coffee beverages, located outside on the south side of the resort.

Sunset Store

Visit our convenience store to purchase snacks, drinks, gifts, and more. We are located next to the resort lobby.

Fitness Center

Get your workout on at our gym, located on the east side of the resort. We have plenty of weight training and cardio equipment.

And More

Other amenities includes a playground, arcade, conference rooms, laundry facilities, free high-speed wi-fi, room service, free parking, and more.

About Us

The Shoto Resort is a beach resort that is located in Magnolia City. Opened in 2014, our beach resort is the place for your next vacation because of our great rooms, amenities, services, and beautiful year-round weather for you and your family.
