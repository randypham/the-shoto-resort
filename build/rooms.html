Rooms

Standard Room

Our standard room comes with 1 king bed and a private balcony with a city view. There is also a full bathroom, a sofa bed, and a kitchenette (microwave, mini fridge, coffee maker, sink). Sleeps up to 4 people.

  • High Speed Wi-Fi
  • Bath Amenities
  • Non-Smoking

Deluxe Room

Our deluxe room comes with 2 queen beds and a private balcony with a partial beach view. There is also a full bathroom, a sofa bed, and a kitchenette (microwave, mini fridge, coffee maker, sink). Sleeps up to 6 people.

About Us
