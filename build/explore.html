Explore

Adventures

Want to take an adventure at the island? Luckily, there are many activities available for you to do. Some of them are scuba diving, jet skiing, hiking, biking, boating, and many more. You will have endless fun at and as well outside the resort.

Restaurants

There are many great places to find delicious food on the island. Eat at any of our restaurants or find some great restaurants around the island. Some great ones are Gray's Seafood Buffet, the Crab Shack, Sunset Grill, and many more.

Shopping

Shopping is a must when going on a vacation. From buying souvenirs to beach essentials, there are endless places to go shopping for your needs. You can visit the large Magnolia Shopping Center dowtown or visit the local beach shops.

Entertainment

There are many activities and events for your entertainment on the island everyday. Some popular events include surfing tournaments, beach volleyball tournaments, beach fireworks, outdoor movies on the beach, concerts, nightclubs, and more.

About Us

The Shoto Resort is a beach resort that is located in Magnolia City. Opened in 2014, our beach resort is the place for your next vacation because of our great rooms, amenities, services, and beautiful year-round weather for you and your family.
